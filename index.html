About

Kim Starkey is a translational education researcher and former middle school teacher. Her work sits at the intersection of what research says and what practitioners actually need — translating between those two languages without flattening either one.

She holds a PhD from the University of Missouri–St. Louis. Her research focuses on teacher preparedness, first-year teacher support, and the systems that shape whether new teachers stay.

HecKte is the practice: Human-Centered Translational Education. The question every project starts with is the same — what's happening here, and who needs to understand it?
